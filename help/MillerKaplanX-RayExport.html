Miller Kaplan X-Ray Export

The Miller Kaplan X-Ray Export generates up to 24 months of billing, by agency, advertiser, and vehicle, in CSV format for exporting to Miller Kaplan. This export is run from the Traffic Exports menu or from the standalone Exports module. Available on version 7.1 and above only.


The available selectivity is the starting year, month, and number of periods. The year and month defaults to the last year and month that was invoiced. A single contract number can also be entered for verification purposes.


The following fields are included on the export: 


  • ClientName: Client Network Name from Traffic Site Options. 
  • Agency: Agency name, or blank if the advertiser is a direct advertiser. 
  • Advertiser: Advertiser name.
  • Product: Product name.
  • Salesperson: First and last name of primary salesperson.
  • Cash/Trade: C or T for Cash or Trade. 
  • Product Code Description: Primary product protection code.
  • AirTime/NTR: Indicates where it is Air Time or NTR.
  • Sales Office: Primary salesperson office.
  • Standard Month: Example: Feb 2019 
  • Gross: Gross billing by advertiser for the month.


Export screen example:



To run the export, simply enter the start year, month, and number of periods, then press the Export button. The export file gets created in the Export folder, with a filename name of MillerKaplan [client abbreviation] followed by the starting and ending month and year (mmyyyy).


The export output can be validated by checking it against the Invoice Register for the matching period for cash and trade, including IN, AN, and HI, for air time and NTR, including political and non-political.
