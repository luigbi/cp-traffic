Planner - Projections

Projections are estimates that can be used to gauge possible future business and follow the progress of negotiations.


Projection revenue can be defined for possible business before a proposal is created, or pulled from a proposal already entered.


Projection revenue is stored on a weekly basis predefined by you.  No projections can be added to a new week until the week has been ‘rolled over’.  This allows you to maintain the weekly revenue integrity for pacing reports.


You must have the Proposal System to use the Projection feature.

Blocking Date

Sales Management decides what the Pacing week for a projection entry will be. Set the Block Date to be the date and time until which salespeople can enter projections. 


To set the date, go to Projections -> Block. Enter the last date and time salespeople will be allowed to enter projections for the week.

This allows the projection data to be stored and run for pacing information.  

Rollover

Once the Block date and time have been reached, Sales cannot enter or change Projection data until the week has been rolled over, or ‘re-opened.’ The Rollover actually stores the entered projected information for that week. This is how the pacing week is created for YTD projection comparisons.



Typically, you rollover a week at a time – for example, Friday at 12pm. That means that any projections that are entered for the week till 12pm will be included on the current report. When you run a past report for the week, it will include the projections added for the past week up to that blocking date and time.


When all Projections reports have been run and analyzed, roll over the block date and time to the next week by clicking the Rollover button and entering the next blocking date and time. This will once again un-block Projections so that projections can be entered until the next blocking date and time has been reached.
