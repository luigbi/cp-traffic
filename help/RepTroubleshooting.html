Rep Troubleshooting

How do I split the revenue for a vehicle so it will go to accounts receivable and history?

The question that determines how the revenue is updated during invoicing is defined in the Sales Source.  The Sales Source has a question, “Invoice Update” and the choices are Receivables, History or Ask.  If you change the option to Ask, you will be able to split the revenue between accounts receivable and history.

How to determine how many spots aired vs. ordered spots for a client?

Print the Rep Invoice Worksheet. This report will show all the spots ordered for the national order and the aired spot counts that were entered or imported.

Invoicing Error

If you receive the following error when you request, “Print Rep Invoices”: “Partial month previously invoiced. Unable to run ‘Print Rep Invoices’ until entire month invoiced”, it means that either air time invoicing was run for the next month or an invoice was added to the month you are trying to run the final Rep invoice for. To fix this problem, generate the final air time and NTR invoices for the month.  You will then be able to Print Rep Invoices

No Data 

If this is the first time that you have run final invoices for Rep and if the invoice form is created but no invoice, go to Site Options. On the Invoicing Tab, look at the option “Print Rep Invoices by…..Market     Vehicle”. The problem may be that Market is selected and the vehicle does not have any Market vehicle group defined. To fix it, change the option to be Vehicle.
