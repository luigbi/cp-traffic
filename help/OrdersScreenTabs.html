Orders Screen Tabs

Directly below the contract header area are tabs that are used to bring up different sections in the lower half of the screen. 


The following tabs may be shown depending on system settings and software versions:


Multimedia: multimedia is used with sports vehicles to track item inventory, the cost of each item, order items by event schedule, and bill for non-air time inventory. Items include tickets, hospitality, signs, etc. See the Multimedia section in this document for more information.


NTR: for entering non-traditional revenue (NTR). See the NTR section for a complete explanation of this tab.


Air Time: the air time tab is where air time vehicles, dayparts, and spots are entered. The majority of this document covers the air time tab. (On version 8.1 and above, this tab is labeled “Air/Pod Spots”, reflecting the fact that it is used for both air time and “podcast spot” lines/baked-in spots.)


Digital: available on version 8.1 and above only, and accessible only when the Ad Server feature is enabled, this tab is used to enter lines for dynamically inserted ads. Digital lines do not have spots, but instead have a start and end date range that is defined for each line, with an impression goal and other values.


Merchandising: when there’s a merchandising comment entered in the contract header area, the merchandising revenue gets allocated on this tab. See the Merchandising/Promotional section in this document for more information.


Promotional: when there’s a promotional comment entered in the contract header area, the promotional revenue gets allocated on this screen. See the Merchandising/Promotional section in this document for more information.


Installment: the installment tab is used when the contract will be paid in installments, or paid in advance. See the Installment section in this document for more information.


Avails and Protection: these two tabs are part of the Advanced Avails feature, and may not be enabled on all systems. For a complete explanation of these two tabs, please see the Advanced Avails feature in this document.
