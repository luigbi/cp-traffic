Entering Projections

Salespeople enter projections in the period of time in which the Projections are not blocked.

Projection Entry Procedure

To enter Projections:

  • Go to the Proposals screen and click the Projection button in the bottom left corner of the screen. 
  • When signed onto the system as a Salesperson, the Salesperson will be automatically selected from the Salesperson dropdown in the upper right corner, and the Sales Office (the first field on the Projections grid) will also be automatically entered, using the Sales Office defined for the Salesperson on the Salespeople List screen.
  • Tab to the Advertiser field and select the advertiser from the advertiser dropdown. If it is a new advertiser, select [new] to add the advertiser to the system.
  • After entering the advertiser, enter the product associated with the proposal in the Product field. 
  • Pressing Tab from the Product field will advance to the Demo (D) field, where the Demo category can be entered. However, if there is a completed proposal for this advertiser, you can go back and select it from the Proposal dropdown. When pressing Tab, a question will appear that asks “Copy totals from Proposal to Projections”. Pressing Yes will copy the information from the Proposal to the Projection. Press No will allow you to continue entering the information manually.
  • Enter the vehicle in the Vehicle field.
  • Indicate whether the business is New or Return in the N/R column.
  • The % column is where you enter the Potential Code. The Potential Code represents the likelihood of the projection becoming an Order. Select a code from the dropdown menu, or enter a new code by selecting New and filling out the required fields.
  • The “C” column is the ‘change reason’, which is mandatory when a projection amount is changed.
  • Finally, enter the projected revenue amount in the calendar area to the right of the Change Reason column. The revenue can be entered by Quarter, Month, or Week by selecting the corresponding radio button at the bottom of the screen (the Total amount will be calculated automatically based). 
  • After entering the Projection information, tab through the line and press Save to save it.

Entering Potential Codes in the Proposal 

You can also enter Projection Codes on Proposals by selecting the Potential Code in the contract header area. If you select this Proposal when entering the Projection, the Potential Code entered in the contract header area will automatically populate in the corresponding Projections field.
