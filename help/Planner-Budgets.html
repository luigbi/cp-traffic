Planner - Budgets

The Budgets screen is really two screens: Budgets and Actuals. 


  • Budgets allow the user to create and manipulate budgets, plans, and forecasts. 
  • Actuals are used to track your actual sales/revenue for pricing to make plan. 


Budgets allows you to:


  • Enter goals by Salesperson, Vehicle, Office, etc.
  • Assign unsold inventory a value based on the average of what has been sold so far for the year.
    • Add the value of the unsold inventory to the contracted prices of the sold inventory, to arrive at a revised budget.
    • Apply a user-supplied estimated percent sellout to the unsold inventory. 
  • Create budgets for subsequent years or revising the budgets throughout the year by 
    • Calculating a year-to-year trend, and extrapolating it to the next year,
    • Apply an index up or down to each advertiser for the upcoming year,
    • Use the system to examine any number of past years and extrapolate the trend up or down by vehicle,
    • Scale each vehicle up or down by a specified index, or to a specified amount.
    • Change the average rate, and/or percent sellout, and/or CPP, and/or CPM for the new year.  
    • The system will calculate the budget for each vehicle based on the entered values.
  • Run reports that utilize any of the budgets, comparing them to actuals for one or many years.

Budget Tools

The Budget tools are used to modify budgets and give you tremendous manipulative power. Each button at the bottom of the screen: Advertiser, Trend, Scale, Research, Actual, 12 Months, and Add Vehicle is designed to alter a budget in a specific way. When modifying budgets, a user can use none, one, some, or all of these buttons. 

Actuals Tools

Only three of the buttons are available for modifying Actuals (distinct from Budgets and entered on the Actuals screen). These are the Actuals button, 12 Months, and Add Vehicle. The other buttons are grayed out when the toggle is set to Actual.
