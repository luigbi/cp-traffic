Web Export Troubleshooting

If spots fail to get exported to the Counterpoint Affidavit System website, it could be due to a variety of causes. The following are steps to help troubleshoot why spots were not successfully exported:


  • Was a log generated in the Traffic System?
    • Check the log dates in the Log Screen and generate the log for the current week if it has not yet been done
  • Did spots get feed into the Affiliate System?
    • Check Network Log in the Affiliate System to verify that the network log for the vehicle has been generated and spots have been transmitted to the affiliate database. This should always be done before checking Affiliate Affidavits, and will tell you whether you need to re-generate the traffic log
    • Go to Affiliate Affidavits and call up the station and vehicle in question. Verify that the week in question exists in either Outstanding or Received
    • Click on the week in question and Click No to the question Did all Spots Air? This will allow you to see the actual spot information
      • Verify that the spots do not have a status of “9 Not Carried”
  • Verify that there is a current agreement between the Station and Vehicle in question
    • In the Agreement Screen, call up the Station and Vehicle (the button above the Vehicle drop down should be defaulted to Affiliated With: Yes). If you do not see the Vehicle, there is no agreement
      • Click on Affiliated with No, to verify that the vehicle is not affiliated with the station
    • If there is an agreement, check the start and end date of the agreement, check the delivery method, and check the terms of the pledge
  • Check email addresses for the failed station
    • In the Station -> Personnel screen, make sure that there are valid email addresses for the station in question
    • Make sure at least one email address has AFFEmail checked on
  • Finally, if no spots export to the Counterpoint Affidavit System, review the WebExportLog.Txt file
    • After every export, successful or unsuccessful, a record of the export is recorded in the WebExportLog.Txt file, found in the messages folder
    • Results from the most recent export will be at the bottom of the report
    • Scroll to the bottom for a list of reasons why an export failed
    • Email the WebExportLog.Txt to support@counterpoint.net using Accessories -> Messages

Export Troubleshooting Reports

There are three Affiliate reports that are helpful when troubleshooting an export issue: Export Journal, Export History, and Export Monitoring.

Export Journal

The Export Journal lists all things associated with the export: vehicles, stations, spot counts, added and deleted spots (in the case of re-exports), the date, time, and duration of the export, and any errors that the export generated. 


The report can be generated to show information for all spots exported or only to show discrepancies.

Export History

The Export History shows a 7-day history and the status of the exports generated. The type of export, number of vehicles exported, as well as the date and start/end times are listed, as well as the user that initiated the export are displayed. Each export’s status, either ‘Completed’ or ‘Error,’ is also listed.

Export Monitoring

The Export Monitoring Report provides you with a list of all vehicles, stations, and markets exported for the week.
