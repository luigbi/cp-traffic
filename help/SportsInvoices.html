Sports Invoices

Once all of your check programs are complete, and any errors fixed, you are ready to generate invoices. The steps for generating a sports invoice are exactly the same as generating conventional invoices.


Select the type of invoices to generate (NTR will automatically be included with Air Time), and whether you wish to run Preliminary or Final invoices. Preliminary is used for proofing, and will not update receivables. Finals will update receivables and advance the systemâ€™s invoice dates. 

Incomplete Live Log Events

If you are using Live Log, any events not Marked as Complete will appear in a white box at the bottom of the screen. 


Resolve any issues and proceed with invoicing by clicking Generate.

Invoice Summary

Sometimes, you may not want an advertiser/agency to see the detailed breakout of an invoice. In these cases, you can run the Invoice Summary report by either detail or summary, to limit the information presented on the invoice. The Invoice Summary is found in the Report screen, and is run after final invoicing has been generated.
