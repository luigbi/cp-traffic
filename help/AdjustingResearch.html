Adjusting Research

Audience Adjustment

On version 7.1 and above, when creating a new research book by modeling from an existing book, the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field can be used to scale the audience numbers from the modeled from book to the new research book, scaling them up or down using a percentage value that you enter. 



When doing this, the original research book numbers are not affected. Only the new research book numbers are scaled. This scaling affects audience numbers for the sold, extra, time, and vehicle settings, pre-defined daypart audience numbers, and custom demo audience numbers.


To scale the audience numbers when modeling from an existing research book, on the “Model from research book” popup that appears when creating a new research book, in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field, enter a positive or negative number that you want to adjust the existing audience numbers by, and then press the OK button. 

For example, to increase the audience numbers by 100%, enter 100 in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field. To decrease the numbers by 10%, enter -10 in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field. Leave a zero or blank value in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field to not adjust the numbers.

Adjust Button

On version 7.1 and above, the Adjust button (located at the bottom of the screen), allows you to scale the audience numbers up or down by vehicle for all the audience categories for a selected research book using a percentage value that you can enter on the “Adjust” popup. This affects audience numbers for the sold, extra, time, and vehicle settings, pre-defined daypart audience numbers, and custom demo audience numbers for the selected book.


To adjust the audience numbers, first select a research book from the research book dropdown, then press the Adjust button. Select one or more vehicles to adjust the numbers for by clicking the vehicle name that appears in the list of vehicles. (You can select more than one vehicle at a time by either ctrl-clicking on a vehicle, or by clicking one vehicle, then pressing shift-click on another vehicle to select all the vehicles between those two vehicles.) In the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field, enter a positive or negative number that you want to adjust the numbers by, and then press the OK button. 



For example, to increase the audience numbers by 100%, enter 100 in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field. To decrease the numbers by 10%, enter -10 in the “+/- Audience % Adjust” field. After adjusting the numbers, press Save to save the changes.
