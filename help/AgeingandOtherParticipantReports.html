Ageing and Other Participant Reports

Ageing by Participant 

The Ageing by Participant report produces an ageing by Participant within Sales Source. Transactions are split by Participants. 


Ageing by Producer 

The Ageing by Producer report generates an ageing sorted by Sales Source within Participant. Transactions are not split by Participants. 


Ageing by Sales Source 

This report is an ageing sorted by Sales Source within Participant. Transactions are split by Participants. 


Ageing by Vehicle 

This ageing report displays each vehicle with all transactions. 


When the Participant Vehicle Group is selected, the owner's percent share of receivables is shown, like the 80% share shown for "NFL Football" and "Acme Rep" in the picture below.


Other Vehicle Groups can be selected to show an A/R for that Subtotal or Sub-company group.

Billed and Booked 

To see every participant and their share of the revenue, you can use the Billed and Booked by Vehicle/Participant. 


If you only want to see the share of a particular participant, select Participant in the Group dropdown and select then select the participants from the two Participant boxes in the selectivity screen. 


When more than two participants are defined for the split, be sure to select all participants. This ensures that the split for the contract/vehicle is calculated for all participants. Each participant will show their share of the revenue. 


The Billed and Booked sort Vehicle Gross/Net shows the owner's share. The owner is the first participant defined for the vehicle. 

Participant Payment Status 

This report is used to show participants how much they have earned. Revenue is reported year to date for both net billed and year to date cash distributed using the entered dates for each participant or owner. Dollars owed, if any, are also shown. 


Vehicle Participant Information Report 

The Vehicle Participant Information report lists vehicles (including optionally dormant vehicles) by type or owner and shows the start/end dates and percentages and invoice update settings defined for participant revenue spits. It can be used for verification purposes.
