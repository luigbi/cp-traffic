Viewing and Printing Logs

Logs can be accessed from the Dashboard by clicking the “Logs and Audio button” for a vehicle, then either pressing "View Entire Week" for a selected week to view the entire week for that vehicle, or by pressing "View Days" then selecting a single day to view.



Color coding is used to indicate logs/affidavits that are outstanding (have not been posted at all), partially completed (have at least one spot posted but not all spots), or completed (all spots are fully posted).

Viewing Logs

The Log is a list of spots that are scheduled to air for the selected vehicle and date range.



The vehicle name is shown near the top of the page, along with the date range being viewed. The spot information is shown in the second part of the screen, sorted by default by pledge date and time, with the following pieces of information appearing for each spot:


  • Advertiser/Product: The name of the advertiser and the product. 
  • ISCI code: The unique identification code for the copy.
  • Comment: If a copy instruction comment is available, a “speech bubble” icon will appear in the “C” column. Click the icon to see the comment, or hold the mouse over the icon to see mouseover text with the comment on it. (This comment will also appear on the printed log when printed and on the affidavit.)
  • Length: The length (in seconds) of the spot.
  • Pledge Date: The date the spot is pledged to air.
  • Pledge Time: The time that the spot is pledged to air.
  • Copy End Date: The last day that the copy is valid to air (using the latest rotation end date).


To switch to a different date or date range, click the date button near the upper right corner.

Additional Log Options

Depending on configuration settings for the vehicle, the avail name, feed dates and times, and cart numbers can also be shown.


When the "Spot Summary" feature is enabled, and the "Avail Name" feature is not enabled, a special Spot Summary is shown near the top of the page that lists a count of each spot for each advertiser/product.



When the "Spot Summary" feature and "Avail Name" feature are both enabled for the vehicle, a different Spot Summary is shown near the top of the page that also groups spots by inside program and outside program categories, as shown in the picture below. Printing Logs

Logs can be printed by pressing the “Print” button. 


When printing the log, the user can choose whether to set the report to print to a printer or to print to PDF by selecting the corresponding option from within the browser.


Printed logs use the selected vehicle, date range, and sort options from the log that is being viewed.
