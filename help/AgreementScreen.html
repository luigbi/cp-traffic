Agreement Screen

To access the Affiliate Agreements screen, click the Affiliate Agreements button from the main menu of the Affiliate System.

Selection Options

Agreements can be entered, viewed and edited by Station and Vehicle, or by Vehicle and Station. 

By Station and Vehicle

By default, stations are available for selecting from the dropdown menu that appears on the left side of the screen. The list of stations in the Station dropdown menu can be sorted alphabetically by call letters (the default), or by DMA, by clicking the DMA radio button. To view an existing agreement, simply select a station from the dropdown menu on the left side of the screen, then select a vehicle from the dropdown menu on the right side of the screen.


The list of vehicles by default includes active agreements for vehicles affiliated with the selected station. To view active and expired agreements, uncheck the “Active” checkbox (an inactive agreement is any agreement that has a “last date on air”, “end date”, or “drop date” prior to today’s date), then select the vehicle from the vehicle dropdown list.


To view vehicles that are not affiliated with the selected station (for the purpose of creating a new agreement), click the “Affiliated: No” radio button, then select the vehicle from the vehicle dropdown list.

By Vehicle and Station

To view agreements by Vehicle and Station, click the “Active Veh” (Active Vehicle) radio button to see all active vehicles, or the “All Veh” (All Vehicle) radio button to see all vehicles (active and dormant) on the left side of the screen. When viewing agreements by Vehicle and Station, the Stations will be selectable from the dropdown on the right side of the screen.


By default, the list of stations includes stations with active agreements that are affiliated with the selected vehicle. To view active and expired agreements, uncheck the “Active” checkbox, then select the station from the station dropdown list. 


To view stations that are not affiliated with the selected vehicle (for the purpose of creating a new agreement), click the “Affiliated: No” radio button, then select the station from the dropdown list. 


The list of stations in this view can also be sorted alphabetically by Call Letters or by DMA by selecting the “By Stations” radio button or the “DMA” radio button on the right side of the screen directly above the Stations dropdown menu.
