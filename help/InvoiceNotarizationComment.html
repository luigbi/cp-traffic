Invoice Notarization Comment

Invoices normally print in alphabetical order by Payee (either agency or direct advertiser). If you have certain agencies and advertisers who always require notarization, you can cause their invoices to always sort to the top of the invoice stack, so you don’t have to hunt for them (see step 1 below).


If you wish to add the notarization text to an invoice, you must create it as Boilerplate, and then add it to each desired proposal or order (see steps 2 and 3 below).

Setup agencies and/or advertisers to sort to the front of the invoice stack

Agencies
  1. For agencies that demand notarization, go to the List screen.
  2. Click on the Agency button.
  3. Select the desired agency.
  4. Click on the “Invoice Sort” field, and select Notarization Needed.
  5. This will cause all invoices for that agency to sort to the front of the invoice pile. For easy identification, they will have “Notarization needed” printed on the bottom of the Invoice.

Advertisers
  1. For advertisers that demand notarization, go to the List screen.
  2. Click on the Advertiser button.
  3. Select the desired advertiser.
  4. Click on the “Invoice Sort” field, and select Notarization Needed.
  5. This will cause all invoices for that advertiser to sort to the front of the invoice pile.  For easy identification, they will have “Notarization needed” printed on the bottom of the Invoice.

Create a Boilerplate Comment

  1. Go to the List screen.
  2. Click on the Boilerplate button.
  3. NEW is the default in the upper right dropdown.
  4. Tab to “Type”, and select Other Comment
  5. Tab to “Title”, and enter Notarization
  6. Tab to “On Proposal” and select NO
  7. Tab to “On Order” and select NO
  8. Tab to “On Spot/Copy Screen” and select NO
  9. Tab to “On Invoice” and select YES
  10. Tab to “Comment” and enter the full text that should appear on an invoice that needs Notarization.  Include underline characters to allocate spaces for written data and signatures.
  11. Proof read data, and correct any errors.
  12. Press SAVE button
  13. Press DONE button



Enter the Notarization text on an order

New Orders

  1. Enter order header data.
  2. The last field in the header is “Other Comments”.  When you tab to it, NONE will be displayed.
  3. Press the dropdown in the “Other Comments” field, select Comment, and Tab.
  4. The Comment entry screen will appear.
  5. In the upper left, click on Notarization.  The text of the Notarization comment will appear in the box in the upper right.
  6. Press the Move button to move the Notarization text into the blue box.
  7. You may change any of the text in the blue box if you wish.
  8. Tab to save the Comment, and move on to entry of schedule lines.
  9. When you print invoices, the Notarization text will automatically appear at the base of the form.


Existing Orders

  1. Access the desired order.
  2. Go straight to the “Other Comments” field and click on it.
  3. Follow steps 3 to 8 from above, then save the order.
  4. Do not increment the modification number, unless you are making other changes at the same time.
  5. Important: After saving the order, you must press the Schedule button at the bottom left of the screen. When the “Contract Scheduling” screen appears, select the contract to which you are adding the Notarization text, and press Schedule. Until you do that, the text will not appear on any invoices for that order.
