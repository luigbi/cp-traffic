Rep Traffic Procedure

  1. Enter contracts with vehicles that have been defined as Rep Vehicles. Note: Rep Vehicles cannot be intermixed with other vehicle types on the contracts.
  2. Rep vehicles can be entered as virtual packages, but the invoicing will invoice the rep vehicle, not the package vehicle.
  3. Schedule lines can be daily or weekly buys. (Site Option question)
  4. Other Comments and Cancellation Comments will show on the printed order and the invoice.
  5. Other Comments can also print on the insertion order if there are generalized instructions.
  6. Line Comments print on the insertion order and can be used for any specialized instructions to the station.
  7. NTR billing can be entered on Rep Orders.
  8. Print Insertion Orders for the Affiliates to tell them what to air
    1. Other Comments print on all insertion orders.
    2. Line Comments print on insertion orders for the associated vehicle
    3. Rates can be suppressed
    4. Rates can show net-net spot value
  9. Rep vehicles show on management reports such as the Billed and Booked Report.
  10. At the end of the month update your accounts receivable for the month.  Run the Gen Rep A/R option from the Invoice Job.
  11. Run revenue reports to close the period.  Print your Invoice Register by Invoice Number with summary totals.  The report will consist of all ordered revenue, less any previous month adjustments.
    1. Invoice Register by Invoice Number with summary totals will give you one line per contract.
    2. Invoice Register by Airing Vehicle has the ability to give totals by Vehicle Group.
  12. Follow normal Collections Procedures as defined in the Traffic & Accounting Manual.  Close the period.
  13. Post the aired spots from the station affidavits in Post Log as they are received.
  14. Weekly, generate Final Invoices to create invoices for those contracts that have been completely posted.
  15. A Statement for current month items only can be generated to go along with the invoices, summarizing all invoice and adjustment transactions for the advertiser.
  16. Request the Unbillable Invoices Report as needed to determine how much revenue is still outstanding to post. When this report shows nothing for a month, that month has been completely billed.
  17. Request the Delinquent Affidavits Report.  This report is used to contact the affiliate for outstanding affidavits for the month selected.  The contact information defined for the vehicle is shown on this report.
  18. To review the ordered vs. posted spot counts for a Market, the Rep Invoice Worksheet will show the counts in detail for each station or summary by contract.
