Collections Troubleshooting

How do I put an account In Collection?

Select the account on the Collection Screen. Change the Payment Rating to be ‘In Collection’.


How do I find an invoice?

Select the Invoice Button. Enter the invoice number and select the find button.  If the invoice is in the current or history A/R, it will show as well as any other transactions with that invoice number.


What do I do if the invoice is in history?

Change the Type of Account information from Cash to Cash History. Change the Action from View to Restore. Mouse click on the invoice and you will see the lines that zero balance that invoice change color. The balance due should be $.00.  Save will move the transactions from History to the current A/R.  


How do I find my payment on account transactions?

Select the Aging by Vehicle, show totals by ‘detail’. Display the report. All PO transactions will sort to the front of the report and you can print those pages of PO transactions. Refer to Cash Management chapter for additional information about payments on account.


I have a check that bounced and I don’t see it in the Collections Screen for the account.

The account may have been moved to history with Zero Purge. Change the Type of Account information from Cash to Cash History to see if the check is there. Use the Cash History Restore action to move it back into the current A/R.


I need to have some clients send their checks to one address and other clients to send them to another address. 

You can define two Lock Box addresses and assign the appropriate lock box to each payee.


I have one agency, John Doe, but he is set up in different locations.  How do I define it so each order goes to the right office?

You would define an agency for each location. Use the City ID to identify which location they are from.  


How do I apply an adjustment to one producer for an invoice?

You need to add an adjustment and change the toggle to Air Vehicle. This will list an amount due next to each invoice transaction that you can use to apply your adjustment.


Where do I change a client’s Credit to Approved?

There are two places.  You can change the payee or the advertiser credit approval in the List- Advertiser/Agency screen, or you can use the Collection Screen. When using the Collection Screen, you can change it for the Payee or use the “Statistics for” to change to the advertiser to see their credit to change.


I forgot to add my transaction comment.

If you forget to enter the comment, there is no way to go back and add it on the existing transaction. What you could do is to enter another transaction for $.00 with the comment.


I’m not sure if Zero Purge was run for the month.  How can I check?  
Go to List – Site Options for Accounting. The Last Purged Date is shown there. 


My Payment on Account has been posted to the wrong payee.

If you accidentally posted the Payment on Account to the wrong Payee, you can use the Transfer Action to move the PO transaction.
