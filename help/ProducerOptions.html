Producer Options

The Affiliate ISCI Export produces a file that contains the station, advertisers and unique ISCI codes.  This is a customized list for each station of the commercials they need to air.  When a log is altered after export to the website, an alert is issued to rerun the Log export, and an alert issued to rerun the ISCI export.


  • Enter the producer information for the vehicle
  • When the commercials are embedded in the program, enter the Producer and a Commercial Content Provider.  Enter Yes in the Commercial Embedded entry
  • When the commercials are not embedded, define a Commercial Content Provider and enter No in the Commercial Embedded entry
  • Commerical Content Provider: You must enter the Commercial Content Provider’s Information  if you are using unique ISCI’s by Vehicle in the ISCI export
  • If not defined, station information will be included in the output
  • The commercial content provider’s abbreviation is what will be used in the Export file name
  • If you have been given a Program ID from your Audio Provider, enter it in Vehicle Options->Interface. Click the ISCI toggle until it says H#B#, this will allow the Program ID field to be used and an alphanumeric code to be entered. If you do not, you can only enter numbers in the Vehicle ID field

Producer Specifications

Name:  1 to 40 alphanumeric characters.

Abbreviation:  1 to 10 alphanumeric characters.

Address:  1 to 25 alphanumeric characters

E-Mail Address:  1 to 70 alphanumeric characters

FTP Address:  1 to 70 alphanumeric characters

Send ISCI To:  Hard coded answer of Manual

Web Site Address:  1 to 70 alphanumeric characters

Contact Name:  1 to 40 alphanumeric characters

Phone Number:  1 to 30 alphanumeric characters

Fax Number:   1 to 20 alphanumeric characters
