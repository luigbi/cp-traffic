Installment Invoice Examples

This section shows example installment invoices where the installment billing is allocated across several months. 


On this example, the contract runs in December and January, with the installment amounts evenly distributed across both months of the contract.


When the billing for an installment contract spans multiple months, each invoice will be numbered “Installment X of Y” in the header, where X is the number of the current invoice and Y is a count of the total number of invoices that will be created for this installment contract.
