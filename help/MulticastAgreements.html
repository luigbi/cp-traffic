Multicast Agreements

If you have a station that is set up to be Multicast, the pledges only need to be defined for one of the stations, and the pledges will be automatically copied to each of the selected Multicast stations.


To create a Multicast agreement, select a station that is Multicast (the word “Multicast” will appear next to the station in the Station dropdown), and select the vehicle for which you wish to create an agreement. Once all of the necessary information is entered, click on the Pledge Tab. The stations that are part of a Multicast group with the station you are currently viewing will appear across the top, as well as whether or not they have an agreement for the currently selected vehicle.


Select which stations in the multicast group you want this agreement to copy to, or select all, by clicking directly on the Call Letters. A selected station will display a gray background to indicate that it is part of the multicast group for that vehicle. 


When saving the agreement, new agreements will be created for the selected stations in addition to the station that the agreement was being entered for. 


When you next enter the pledge tab for any of the Multicast stations, you will see the dates of the multicast agreements in the multicast field, as shown in the screenshot below.

Multicast Market and Owner

On version 7.1 and above, on the Pledge tab, for multicast station agreements, the multicast area at the top of the Pledge tab is larger and also shows the station owner (to the right of the market). In addition, the market and owner font will appear in red if it doesn’t match the selected station’s market and owner.

DMA Example: KJJJ-FM and KJJJ-AM are in a multicast group. KJJJ-FM is in the DMA of Los Angeles, and KJJJ-AM is Anaheim. When viewing the Pledge tab for station KJJJ-FM, because the DMA doesn’t match with KJJJ-AM, the DMA will be shown in red in the multicast area for KJJJ-AM. 


Owner Example: KJJJ-FM and KJJJ-AM are in a multicast group. KJJJ-FM is defined with Owner A, and KJJJ-AM is defined with Owner B. When viewing the Pledge tab for KJJJ-FM, because the owners don’t match, the owner for KJJJ-AM will be shown in red in the multicast area for KJJJ-AM.
