Distribution Reports

The Billing and Cash Distribution reports are used to calculate the distribution amounts due to participants based on the defined participant percentages.

Billing Distribution

The Billing Distribution report produces a list of the revenue that each participant group should expect to receive based on what has been invoiced. The report shows each transaction and total share due the participant for the invoicing period selected. Totals can show summary or detail, with the detail version including each contract. 



The following examples illustrate how the report distributes the billing amount for a single invoice to two different participants, with 80% for "Acme Rep" (using the Revenue Percentage defined for the "Phil Collins" vehicle from the Participant tab), and 20% for "XYZ", making up the full 100% of the revenue for the vehicle for this example invoice. 


Cash Distribution 

The Cash Distribution Report when run by Participant shows the distribution amount due for each participant's percentage based on the cash posted. This report uses cash posting dates entered by the user, which should be tracked so that non-overlapping dates are always used. The report can also be run by invoice and check number.


These examples shows all cash entered between 12/28/15 and 1/10/16, with the first picture illustrating the amount due to "Acme Rep" using the 80% participant split defined for the vehicle/sales source/participant, and the second picture showing the amount due to "XYZ" using the 20% participant split.
