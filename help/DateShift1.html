Date Shift

The Dates button is used to shift the dates of contract lines on a new contract, using the left and right arrows shown at the top of the screen. 


To shift dates:

  1. Select the line or lines you wish to shift the dates for.
  2. Click the left or right arrows to adjust the start date of the line.
  3. The new line start date will shift accordingly.
  4. Press Done when finished to return to the main screen.

Remove Weeks

On version 7.1 and above, when pressing the Dates button, there will be two options: “Shift Dates”, and “Remove Weeks”. The “Shift Dates” option works as described above. The “Remove Weeks” option is used to remove specific weeks from multiple contract lines quickly and easily. (If the entire line is being canceled, use the End button to cancel the line, but if only specific weeks are being canceled, use the Remove Weeks screen.) 


When Remove Weeks is selected, it will list the contract lines, with the line number, vehicle (package vehicle only for packages), daypart, spot length, line date range, and on the right, the contract weeks that are valid for removal. (See picture below for example.)


To remove weeks: 

  1. Select the line or lines to change from the line area.
  2. Select the week or weeks to remove from the “Remove” section (you can use ctrl-click and shift-click to individually pick weeks or select a span of weeks). 
  3. Press Done to remove the selected weeks.
