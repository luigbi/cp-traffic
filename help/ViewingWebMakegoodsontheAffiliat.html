Viewing Web Makegoods on the Affiliate System

Missed spots, missed reasons (if applicable), and Makegoods that have been imported back to the Affiliate System using the Counterpoint Affidavit import will be viewable throughout the Affiliate System.

Affiliate Affidavits

Posted and imported affidavits can be viewed on the Affiliate Affidavits screen. Makegood spots will be shown with the letters "MG" in the "Status" field.


Hold the mouse over a makegood spot to see the missed date and time and missed reason (if applicable). You can also hold the mouse over a missed spot to see the makegood date and time (if it was made good) and the missed reason (if applicable).

Affiliate Management

Makegood spots are also shown on the Affiliate Management screen and are indicated with the word "Makegood" in the "Comment" field.

Affiliate Spot Reports

Affiliate spot reports such as the Fed vs. Aired Clearance report and the Pledged vs. Aired Clearance report also show missed spots and makegoods.


Check on the “Show Status Codes” option to see the makegood status.


The Affiliate spot reports that include makegoods are the following: Advertiser Clearance, Advertiser Fulfillment, Advertiser Placement, Affiliate Clearance Counts, Affiliate Spot Management, Fed vs. Aired Clearance, Pledged vs. Aired Clearance, and the Spot Clearance report.


The Affiliate Spot Management Report was specifically designed to review unresolved missed spots, and resolved missed spots (makegoods).
