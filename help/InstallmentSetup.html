Installment Setup

Site Options

Activate Installment Billing (Air time and NTR are automatically combined when installment billing is selected) from the Options tab, System Options.

Traffic Site Options->Accounting

There are two different Installment Methods available in Traffic Site Options. 


Choose the method of reporting revenue on the Accounting tab. Once chosen, this method cannot be changed.

  • Invoiced (Inv is Rev): This will report your revenue as it billed. For example, if the flight goes for twelve months, but the entire contract is billed upfront in January, all of the revenue will report in January.
  • Aired (Separate Inv from Rev): This will report your revenue as aired. For example, if the flight goes for twelve months, but they are billed upfront in January, the revenue will report across the twelve months as aired.


Example: If you have a contract that has $100 ordered per month for 5 months ($500 total) and the client wants one $250 invoice in March and May.
