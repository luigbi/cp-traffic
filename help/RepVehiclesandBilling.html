Rep Vehicles and Billing

A Rep vehicle is a vehicle that has no inventory or spots. Rep vehicles show on orders, revenue projections, Rate Card and Insertion Orders.  


Acquisition Cost allows you to enter that cost of doing business against a vehicle, as well as the price the advertiser rate. The Billed & Booked, Advertiser Units Ordered and Network-Station Report reflect the acquisition cost vs. the client cost of the spots.  


At the end of the month, A/R person generates the Rep Revenue. This allows you to report the ‘as ordered’ revenue for the billing month and allows the Accounts Receivable Department to close the period. As Affidavits are received from the stations, the aired spots are ‘posted’.  


When Final invoices are generated, for those contracts that have all the affidavits posted, an invoice will be generated. Invoice adjustments will be created automatically if needed if the invoiced amount differs than the ordered Rep Revenue. You may also want to send your client Statements which will summarize all the invoice items. Final invoices can be requested any time.


The Delinquent Affidavits Report will list all the stations, the station contact and telephone number for those affidavits that have not yet been received for an advertiser. The Unbillable Invoices Report lists any outstanding contracts/revenue that has not been billed for the period because the affidavits are missing.
