Podcast Invoices

Invoices will show separate sections for air time/podcast spot lines, and Digital lines.

Air Time and Podcast Spot Lines

The air time/podcast spot lines will have an ordered and aired section, as shown below, and can be configured to show “week of” dates instead of specific air date.

Digital Lines

The Digital section shows Digital lines, with the line ID, vehicle, ad location, price type, targeting definition, impressions delivered for the month, CPM (for CPM buys), and total amount. For CPM lines, the total amount is the impressions delivered for the month multiplied by the CPM divided by 1,000. 


Line Dates

Line dates are shown under the vehicle name for each digital line item. A start and end date is shown, in this format mm/dd/yy-mm/dd/yy. The rules for the dates are as follows (applies to both Flat Rate and CPM price type lines):


  • If the line start date is prior to the start of the invoice period, then it shows the start of the invoice period as the start date. If the line start date is equal or after the start of the invoice period, then it shows the line start date as the start date.
  • If the line end date is after the end of the invoice period, then it shows the last day of the invoice period as the end date. If the line end date is equal or prior to the end of the invoice period, then it shows the line end date as the end date.
  • If the line start date and line end date are the same day (one day), then it shows both the line start date and line end date.


Flat Rate Lines

For Flat Rate lines the total amount shown on the invoice is the monthly cost for the line. The CPM is not shown for Flat Rate lines, and the impressions delivered are irrelevant.

Over Delivery of Impressions

The Site Options setting “Bill Over-Delivered CPM Impressions” determines how the invoice program handles impressions over the impression goal for a line. 

When “Bill Over-Delivered CPM Impressions” is not checked on, only impressions up to the impression goal for a line will be invoiced. For example, if the impression goal was 50,000, and 60,000 were achieved, a maximum of 50,000 impressions would be invoiced. When running invoices, over-delivered impressions are noted on a log file “ContractOverDelivered”, which shows the contract number, ID number, total impressions, and impression goal, like the example below.


Contracts Over-Delivered CPM Impression as of 4/9/21 9:54:05AM

Contracts 65 ID 1 Total Impressions 105000 Impression Goal 100000


When “Bill Over-Delivered CPM Impressions” is checked on, then impressions that are achieved that are greater than the impression goal for the line will also be invoiced. For example, if the impression goal was 100,000, and 120,000 impressions were achieved, the full 120,000 impressions would be invoiced.
