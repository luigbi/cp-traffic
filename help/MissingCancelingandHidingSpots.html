Missing, Canceling, and Hiding Spots

Spots can also be set to missed, canceled, or hidden on the Post Log screen by dragging the spot to the Missed/Cancel/Hidden area on the left side of the screen. By default, the missed reason is shown in this area. Click the blue toggle to toggle between Missed, Cancel, and Hidden.

How to move an aired spot to missed

  1. Hold the left mouse button down on the aired spot until you see the word “drag” appear.
  2. Drag the spot over the missed reason and release the mouse button.
  3. The missed spot will be shown in the missed area with a count of the number of missed spots for that advertiser and contract.


How to cancel a spot

  1. Click the “missed” toggle in the missed reason area in the bottom left to change it to “cancel”.
  2. Drag the spot on top of a cancel reason and then release the mouse button.
  3. The spot will appear in red on the grid at the bottom of the screen.


How to hide a spot

  1. Click the “missed” toggle and change it to “hidden”.
  2. Drag the spot on top of “[Hide]”.
  3. Release the mouse button.  
  4. The spot will appear in light blue on the grid at the bottom of the screen.
